What Does Nonbinary Mean?

Non-binary gender identity is just one term used to describe individuals who may experience a gender identity that is neither exclusively male or female or is in between or beyond both genders.

Note: Non-binary individuals may identify as gender fluid, agender (without gender), third gender, or something else entirely

(verywellmind.com)

Dear Reader

Hello Reader,
The name of the project “We the People” is a nod to the first lines of the Constitution because nonbinary people have been outcasted for who they are even though they are people. It is important to learn about things you don’t understand. As humans, we tend to fear what we don’t understand. So we respond with aggression. You don’t need to agree with everything that is stated in this project but try and keep an open mind. I chose nonbinary people because it’s something that I didn’t have much knowledge about and I wanted to know more about them. How do nonbinary people challenge societal gender norms? Nonbinary people challenge societal gender norms by making gender a spectrum instead of binary. In this project, you will arguments for both why gender is a spectrum and why it isn’t but also you see these people as what they are. People who are living their lives.

Please Enjoy, Donyae

Self Assessment

What surprised me the most was the information I learn and who is actually nonbinary. Which is pretty cool to find out. Like Gerard Way who was the lead singer for My Chemical Romance. I knew almost nothing about nonbinary people and I wanted to learn more about them. The more I did research the more I understood. I learned that nonbinary people have been around for a long time. And I understood how people associate gender with sex. And since there are only 2 sexes(male, female) there must only be 2 genders. I also understand that the argument isn’t “is gender a spectrum” it’s more about the definition of what gender is. People who argue that gender is a spectrum is using gender as to how you present you socially and in society. But people who argue that gender isn’t a spectrum is using gender as a synonym for sex.
